Our History

Neha Films Patna was founded in 1985 by Mr. Anil Kumar Sinha. A passionate filmmaker with a vision of creating powerful and meaningful stories. Starting out as a small production company, Neha Films quickly made a name for itself in the regional film scene, producing critically acclaimed short films and serials. With a focus on storytelling that reflects the diversity of the human experience, Neha Films has become a leading voice in the Indian film industry. Today, Neha Films continues to push the boundaries of film making, leveraging the latest technology and techniques to tell stories that move and inspire audiences around the world.

Our Vision

"Our Vision at NEHA FILMS is to tell stories that inspire and move audiences around the world. We strive to push the boundaries of film making, using the latest technology and techniques to bring fresh perspectives to the screen. Our goal is to create films that entertain, educate, and provoke thought, while staying true to our core values of creativity, collaboration, and excellence. We are committed to fostering a culture of diversity and inclusion in the film industry. We believe that by constantly challenging ourselves and pushing the limits of what is possible, we can create films that will be remembered for generations to come."
